Privacy Policy

At Atomcode, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data during our business transactions.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your name, contact information, and project-specific information when you inquire about our services or become a client. We only use this information to provide you with a customized service and to communicate with you regarding your project.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide, improve, and personalize our services
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Communicate project-related updates and deadlines
  • Send promotional materials (if you have opted in)

Payment Terms

To ensure clarity and mutual commitment, we require a 50% advance payment before starting any project. This initial payment will be used to cover the planning and design phase. The remaining 50% will be due upon project completion.

Fixed Time & Price

Once the scope, time, and pricing of the project are agreed upon, these details will remain fixed. The price and time frame will not change unless there are significant changes to the project scope, which will be discussed and agreed upon in writing.

Project Revisions

We strive to ensure your satisfaction. If, after the project is completed, you encounter any issues or problems, you can request revisions. You are eligible for a revision or change within 10 days of project completion, provided the issue is related to the original agreement. After this 10-day period, additional charges may apply for further changes.

Data Protection

Atomcode takes all necessary steps to protect your personal data. We will not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties without your consent unless required by law.

Changes to This Policy

Atomcode reserves the right to update this privacy policy. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated date will be indicated at the top.
